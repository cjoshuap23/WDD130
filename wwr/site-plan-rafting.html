Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to make accessible all rafting products for online purchase(build revenue). Idaho residents will be able to purchase our products online and a USPS request for shipment will be made for home delivery. This will greatly increase the success of our company because the range of our customers will reach online buyers.

Audience

The target audience is families in Idaho. The target age is twenty to fifty years-old. The colors and fonts in our site will create an adventure theme that will catch the attention of online buyers interested in white-water rafting. We are looking for those who have a steady income and love the thrill of nature. They who want equipment and accessories to be able to go rafting. We expect people to access our site on a desktop or laptop.
